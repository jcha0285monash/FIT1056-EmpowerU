
Course: Information Security

Course Content:
Week 1: Introduction to Information Security
- What is information security?
- Key principles: Confidentiality, Integrity, Availability (CIA Triad)

Week 2: Common Threats and Vulnerabilities
- Malware (viruses, worms, Trojans)
- Phishing and social engineering
- Ransomware attacks

Week 3: Cryptography
- Introduction to encryption and decryption
- Symmetric vs. asymmetric encryption
- Digital signatures and certificates

Week 4: Network Security
- Firewalls and intrusion detection systems (IDS)
- Virtual Private Networks (VPNs)
- Secure protocols (HTTPS, SSL/TLS)

Week 5: Access Control and Authentication
- Multi-factor authentication (MFA)
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Identity and access management

Week 6: Security Policies and Compliance
- Developing security policies
- Compliance standards (ISO, GDPR, HIPAA)

Week 7: Incident Response and Disaster Recovery
- Steps for responding to security breaches
- Backup strategies and disaster recovery plans

Week 8: Future of Information Security
- Emerging threats (AI-driven attacks, quantum computing risks)
- Security in cloud computing and IoT
